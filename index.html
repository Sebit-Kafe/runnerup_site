Soaring Beyond Professions

Cheza chini, just making ends meet,
Hustling through life, never tasting defeat.
Not bound by titles, not caged by fate,
I do anything—call it great!

You once dreamed to touch the skies,
A pilot’s wings, ambition that flies.
Yet here you are, grounded yet free,
Piloting life with mastery.

A win is a win, hakuna kubahatisha,
Steps so steady, hakuna kujikosea.
Photos ama clips, all captured so bright,
But that one video clip remains out of sight.
You play coy with a tease, keeping it close,
Leaving me in suspense, a secret nobody knows.

Farida Writes, pen like a flame,
Wewe ni nuru, keep shining the same.
Success is yours, kwa bidii na dua,
May baraka follow, na riziki ipatikane bila shaka.

So keep soaring, bila kizuizi,
With every dream, weka msingi.
A future unwritten, yet destined to shine,
Farida, this journey is brilliantly thine.
